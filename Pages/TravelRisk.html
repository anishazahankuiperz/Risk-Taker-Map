A quick guide to specific requirements and
recommendations for safe travel in Ukraine

Almost all countries in the world have issued a ‘Do Not Travel’ advisory to their citizens for Ukraine due to active armed conflict and/or their limited diplomatic/military ability to help there. These places are dangerous and by visiting you may be putting yourself at extreme risk

However, Ukraine’s Ministry of Foreign Affairs allows Entrance and Exit into Ukraine for international citizens who are travelling for work, volunteering, and even tourism. If you do travel, it is important for you to understand the travel risks, and to take significant measures to mitigate those risks.

In this section we will provide a quick guide to specific requirements and recommendations for safe travel in Ukraine.

If you are in Ukraine, you should follow the instructions of local authorities at all times, including air raid warning sirens, curfews and evacuation orders. Our travel advice helps you to make informed decisions when you are planning your trip.
