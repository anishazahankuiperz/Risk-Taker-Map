Itinerary Tracking…

(including 24/7 Safety Check-in)

Enabling employers to fulfil their moral and legal, duty of care obligations to their travelling staff.

Based on our experience of tracking thousands of trips across Ukraine, Inherent Risks has developed an accurate and holistic tracking solution that is fit-for-purpose and is effective even when others fail.

Seeing these duty of care and travel risk management failures reported time and again, we worked closely with clients’ to understand their frustrations, set expectations, and develop a solution that is proven to work across Ukraine, based on several data points, including passive and active tracking.

Our medical and security experts maintain an intelligence-led and data-driven Ukraine Risk Map which accurately plots key points of interest, security and safety incidents, health epidemics, travel disruptions and live Member locations throughout Ukraine, and its surrounding borders.

If an incident, threat or disruption occurs, a proactive, locationspecific, and human-verified Alert is sent to Members, via email or message, with an immediate Advisory which recommends the relevant actions to take to mitigate any risk to life and health..

Individual and group trips are monitored 24/7 by qualified and experienced, medical and security operations staff.

Safety Check-In…

(Unlimited, Multi-Trip Check-Ins)

Clients’ can register their trip online, or via their travel administrator. A link detailing how to do this will be emailed to you upon purchase of our Membership, or as an insurance policyholder with the Underwriters’ that Inherent Risks are retained by.

Inherent Risks works with each organisation and individual to find a solution that fits their operational requirements, while also respecting and adhering to operational security requirements. While the option to ‘Check-In’ is provided, for multiple trips per day if required, it is also not a necessity. We work with foreign governments, defence contractors, war crimes investigators, journalists, NGOs and and visiting dignitaries to name a few. We understand the need for confidentiality, especially in such a challenging environment as Ukraine.

Our end-to-end Travel Tracking and Safety Check-In service enables organisations to ensure that trips are monitored, dangers and disruptions are alerted, calls for help are answered, and to ensure compliance with travel policies, warnings are issued when employees travel outside an agreed risk parameter.
