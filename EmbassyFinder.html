Comprehensive list of Foreign Embassies
and Consulates located in Ukraine

There are over 50 Foreign Embassies and 46 Consulates placed in the territory of Ukraine.

Due to the ongoing conflict, some countries have not returned their diplomatic and administrative staff to the country. For a majority, their phone numbers and email addresses are still monitored remotely

In this list, you can connect with a specific Embassy or Consulate in Ukraine to register your presence in the country, and/or request assistance

Most countries, if not all, have advised their citizens not to travel to Ukraine. Even if you request consular assistance, it is not guaranteed. You are therefore advised to purchase appropriate travel insurance that is fit-for-purpose should you need medical and/or security assistance.
