War Risks Data…

Inherent Risks has spearheaded a Multilateral Data Sharing Agreement between several Government of Ukraine agencies and participating Lloyd’s and Company Market Insurers.

In addition, we aggregate intelligence from reliable and verified open source data and reporting, including satellite imagery, emergency and rescue service reports, and live claims data.

Through our proprietary platform, we have logged detailed data on war-related incidents and locations, dated from when Russia invaded Ukraine on 24th February, 2024 to Present Day.

For security reasons, data has been omitted on military targets. Data is only collected for territories under Ukrainian control

A&H Claims Data…

Since February 2022, when Russia invaded Ukraine, Inherent Risks has kept an Incident Register of all insured and uninsured incidents where an international or local, employee or volunteer, of a foreign company or organisation was either injured or killed.

In addition, we have kept a Register of all people insured through our underwriter clients’ who have travelled within the Green, Orange and Red Zones.

From that data we have developed a live Heat Map, integrated with real-time global intelligence and claims cost data, which enables us to better inform clients' of increased risk locations and activities, and better enables underwriters' to price location and industry specific risks across Ukraine.
